Overview

The Mercedes-Benz A-Class is a compact luxury car known for its stylish design, advanced technology, and efficient performance.

he Mercedes-Benz A-Class Sedan combines elegance, performance, and cutting-edge technology.

  • Top Speed: The top speed is electronically limited to 130 mph (210 km/h) for safety.
  • Acceleration: It can go from 0 to 60 mph in about 4.8 seconds
  • Exterior Design: The A-Class sedan boasts a sleek, sporty exterior with aerodynamic lines.
  • Driver Assistance Technologies: Equipped with advanced features: like adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping assist.
  • Interior and Infotainment: Inside, enjoy a premium interior with modern infotainment including MBUX, a touchscreen interface

The average price of a new Mercedes-Benz A-Class sedan in the UK typically starts from around £35,000 to £42,000

The Mercedes-Benz A-Class sedan is positioned as an entry-level luxury vehicle, typically offering good value for its segment.
